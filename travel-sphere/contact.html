Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book? ▼
We recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks in advance for popular destinations, and 3–6 months for peak travel seasons like Christmas, New Year, and summer. Last-minute deals are sometimes available but selection is limited.
What's your cancellation policy? ▼
Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund. Between 15–29 days: 75% refund. Between 7–14 days: 50% refund. Within 7 days: non-refundable. Specific policies may vary by package.
Do I need travel insurance? ▼
We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance for every trip. We partner with leading insurers to offer competitive rates. Every booking includes 24/7 emergency assistance.
Can you customise a package for me? ▼
Absolutely. Our expert travel consultants can create a completely bespoke itinerary tailored to your interests, budget, travel dates, and travel style. Contact us to get started.
What's included in your packages? ▼
Each package specifies exactly what's included — typically flights, hotel accommodation, transfers, guided tours, and some meals. Check the "What's Included" section on each package page for exact details.
